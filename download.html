Maven Repository

Pi4J will be deployed to Maven Central when version 1.0 is released.\

Snapshots will be hosted in the followibng Sonatype OSS repository.

<repositories>
	<repository>
		<id>oss-snapshots-repo</id>
		<name>Sonatype OSS Maven Repository</name>
		<url>https://oss.sonatype.org/content/groups/public</url>
		<snapshots>
			<enabled>true</enabled>
			<updatePolicy>newer</updatePolicy>
		</snapshots>
	</repository>
</repositories>

The following dependency is all that is needed to include Pi4J. 

<dependency>
	<groupId>com.pi4j</groupId>
	<artifactId>pi4j-core</artifactId>
	<version>${project.version}</version>
</dependency>
